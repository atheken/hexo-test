San Franciso Trip

Dec 31, 2014

Katie and I went to San Francisco, CA. IT WAS AWESOME! We walked everywhere, we were both exhausted at the end of every day. One of the reasons we went all the way out to Cali was for Katie to attend the APhA convention (it’s a Pharmacist thing). Cali also has great weather, food, scenery, culture, and cheap cameras/luggage. Katie received an award from the Pharmacy honor fraternity, Rho Chi. This is a national achievement, and I am very proud of her. I will post pictures of our trip soon.
